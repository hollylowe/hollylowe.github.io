Spring Dance Show Organizational Tools

For my senior project I have created a suite of tools to help organize the Cal Poly Spring Dance Show. The tools were made to perfectly fit the system used by student directors each year and should be able to be seamlessly integrated. These tools digitalize a entirely paper system and simplify tasks that take unnecessary amounts of time.

Dancer Form

The Dancer form replaces a paper form that is handed out at the Spring Dance Show information meeting. Instead of the directors handing out 200 papers and then manually typing the information, the dancers can enter their own information into the system with this easy to use form!

Choreographer Form

The Choreographer form has both the proposal form, and the submit dancers form. The proposal form replaces the paper form handed out to choreographers at the choreographer information meeting (which occurs before choreographer auditions). This form allows choreographers to submit their information into the system. The submit dancers form allows choreographers who are in the show to submit the dancer nubmers of the dancers they selected at auditions.
