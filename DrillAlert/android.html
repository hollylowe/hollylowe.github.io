Android Deployment

How to install DrillAlert on your Android device:

(Android 4.3 (API 18) or higher)

Email johnsoncorrigan@gmail.com from a valid google account to request access to the latest release.

Once granted access to the Drill Alert Android App community, click the link provided on the pinned discussion post. On the google play website, click 'BECOME A TESTER' and follow the link to the google play store to download.

Open the application and log in.
Username: CAPSTONE2015\testuser
Password: StartUp!

*If there are any other issues please contact celind@calpoly.edu and johnsoncorrigan@gmail.com.

Access the code for DrillAlert for Android:

The DrillAlert Android App repository is availible here: DrillAlert Android Repository
Download the project open in Android Studio or Eclipe (reccomended).
